Giberia City is the capital city of the Mani Federation and the co-capital city of the Galactic Union of Mani. It is located at the center of the of Giberland Municipality, State of Mars, Second Solar Federation of Mani, Orion District, Galactic Union of Mani. It is also on top of Mt. Olympus. Also, Giberia is famous for its college - the Giberia College of Mani University. The college holds the essense of the ultimate knowledge explored by the great Mani civilization.
