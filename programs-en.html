The Giberia City and the Mani Federation has supported many programs, organizations, and corporations. They include the Demolishing Schools Program (DSP), Moskau Mob, Mani-Cretin Mathematics Association (MCMA), Mani-Cretin Collaboration Organization (MCCO), Giberia College Journal, Green Origin, etc.

DSP and the Moskau Mob

DSP, the Demolishing Schools Program, is a program co-founded by the Demolishment of Schools Organization (DSO), lead by Andrea Zhang, and the Moskau Mob, lead by Giberia von Mars. Its purpose is to annihilate schools and eliminate useless teachers. Sub-organizations of the DSP includes the DSO, Moskau Mob, Eliminating All Useless Teachers (EAUT), Hawaii Dama Daye Tuanhuo (XDDT), GLCPGR (commonly known as the Dognapping Plans), etc. They have successfully demolished Dulwich College Beijing in cooperation with the Galactic Union Financial Affairs Agency (GUFAA) and the Giberland Department of Defense (GDD).

MCCO and MCMA

MCCO, Mani-Cretin Collaboration Organization, is the umbrella organization covering all of collaborations between the Mani Federal Government (Giberia City) and the Cretin Province. The most famous sub-organization under the MCCO is the MCMA, Mani-Cretin Mathematics Association. The MCMA is founded by Tata Li and directed by Kevin Shi and has held many mathematics competitions and activities since 2021. Learn more about the MCMA...

Giberia College Journal

The Giberia College Journal is run by the Giberia College of Mani University. The Founder and Chief Editor of the Giberia College Journal is Tata Li. It is a powerful and reliable (doesn't apply to viewers from Earth) authority of the news media and publishing press of Mani Federation. Recently, the Giberia College Journal has published "The Mani Federation Profile" and "The Document of Classified Plans to Annihilate Schools". Learn more about the Giberia College...

Green Origin

Green Origin is an advanced multinational corporation on Earth that makes great space travel products. The headquarter is located in the Mani Street of Jerusalem, Israel. The company makes cheap rockets that are powered by plasma engines. According to the Giberia College Journal, Green Origin is one of the most influencial company in universe and has generated 1000 googolplex GGC (General Galactic Currency) every day. Their rockets travel at a speed that is faster than light.
